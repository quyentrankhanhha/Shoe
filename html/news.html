Oversized Branding Hits The adidas NMD R1 V2

Did you know the adidas created a 2nd version of the adidas NMD R1? Yup. The shoe debuted in late 2019 and it is known as the adidas NMD R1 V2. Two new colorways for men are showcased above which take on a similar look and are highlighted by bold branding on the tongues. Coming in color options of Core Black/Signal Coral and Cloud White/Signal Coral, these iterations of the NMD R1 V2 feature stretchy and breathable knitted uppers along with synthetic overlays and iridescent three stripes branding on the side panels

Is the Air Jordan 1 Low Varsity Red a Must Cop?

Reflective of the recent Air Jordan 1 Hi 85 “Varsity Red” that dropped back in February, the Air Jordan 1 Low “Varsity Red” is slated to debut soon. Essentially a low-top rendition, the model flaunts a black leather upper with the highlight Varsity Red leather overlays covering its majority. Finally, black nylon tongues with red Jumpman branding, black Wings logo on the heel, followed by a white midsole and red rubber outsole tops off the style.

The Ben & Jerry’s x Nike SB Dunk Low Chunky Dunky Finally Has A Release Date

The highly anticipated Ben & Jerry’s x Nike SB Dunk Low “Chunky Dunky” has finally gotten release information after first surfacing a few weeks ago. Nike Skateboarding has taken the iconic Nike SB Dunk Low and partnered up with giant ice cream brand Ben & Jerry’s to create a colorway inspired by their signature Chunky Dunky ice cream flavor.

Release Date: Takuya Kamioka x Reebok Instapump Fury

Releasing in a couple of days, here is a good look at the Takuya Kamioka x Reebok Instapump Fury. The collab between Reebok and Japanese artist Takuya Kamioka results in a bold iteration of the Instapump Fury. The Takuya Kamioka x Reebok Instapump Fury comes dressed in a Black and Hyper Green color scheme. It starts off with a Black mesh base contrasted by the vibrant Neon Green overlays.

The Vans Mule Pack Transforms The Old Skool And Slip-On

Releasing in a couple of days, here is a good look at the Vans Mule Pack. The Vans Mule Pack includes altered iterations of the Vans Old Skool and Vans Slip-On by removing the heels of each respective model. What you end up getting are mule iterations of each silhouette. For the most part, the Slip-On remains the same, whereas the Old Skool still comes with laces, but since the heel is removed, the laces really have no function.
